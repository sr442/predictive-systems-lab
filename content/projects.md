# Applied Research Projects

Predictive Systems Lab maintains several open-source and proprietary systems that demonstrate our research capabilities in production environments.

## 1. Dynamic Portfolio Optimization Engine

An end-to-end algorithmic trading architecture that autonomously constructs and rebalances portfolios using real-time market data.

- **Techniques**: Mean-Variance Optimization, Black-Litterman models, Risk Parity.
- **Tech Stack**: Python, FastAPI, React, Docker, AWS.
- **Status**: Active Deployment.

## 2. Global Supply Chain Simulator

A probabilistic simulation engine designed to model cascading supply chain failures under geopolitical and climate-related stress events. 

- **Techniques**: Agent-Based Modeling, Monte Carlo Simulation, Network Theory.
- **Tech Stack**: C++, Python, Ray (Distributed Computing).
- **Status**: Closed Beta.

## 3. Macro-Indicator Nowcasting Pipeline

A high-frequency system that continuously ingests alternative data sources (satellite imagery, shipping manifests, sentiment) to predict lagging economic indicators weeks before official release.

- **Techniques**: Dynamic Factor Models, Mixed-Data Sampling (MIDAS), Transformers.
- **Tech Stack**: PyTorch, Apache Kafka, Snowflake.
- **Status**: Research Phase.
