# About Predictive Systems Lab

Predictive Systems Lab is a specialized research institute dedicated to the advancement of forecasting methodologies, probabilistic modeling, and intelligent decision systems.

## Our Mission

We bridge the gap between theoretical statistical learning and applied macroeconomic and industrial forecasting. Our work focuses on developing models that are not just accurate, but calibrated, interpretable, and computationally scalable for high-stakes environments.

## Research Leadership

**Shubhankar Rana**  
*Principal Researcher & Director*

With extensive experience in deployed machine learning, AI-driven portfolio architecture, and distributed systems, Shubhankar leads the lab's initiatives in hybrid forecasting paradigms—combining state-space models with modern deep learning architectures to solve complex sequential prediction problems.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Macroeconomic Prediction**: Developing leading indicators and state-space models for GDP, inflation, and market volatility.
2. **Supply Chain Resiliency**: High-dimensional, hierarchical forecasting for inventory and demand planning under uncertainty.
3. **Financial Time Series**: Feature engineering and signal extraction for active portfolio optimization and risk management.
