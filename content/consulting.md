# Strategic AI & Forecasting Consulting

Predictive Systems Lab partners with forward-thinking enterprises to design, architect, and deploy custom forecasting engines and decision-intelligence systems.

## Services

### 1. Advanced Forecasting Architecture
We design bespoke predictive systems tailored to your unique data topology. From hierarchical demand forecasting for retail to volatility prediction for finance, we construct pipelines that outperform off-the-shelf solutions.

### 2. Probabilistic Risk Modeling
Point forecasts are insufficient for high-stakes decisions. We implement fully probabilistic models that quantify uncertainty, allowing your business to optimize for specific risk tolerances and confidence intervals.

### 3. Model Auditing & Optimization
We evaluate existing predictive infrastructure for data leakage, calibration drift, and computational inefficiency, providing actionable roadmaps for architectural modernization using MLOps best practices.

## Engagement Model

Our engagements are structured as technical partnerships. We work directly with your engineering and data science teams to ensure knowledge transfer and seamless integration of predictive intelligence into your production environment.
